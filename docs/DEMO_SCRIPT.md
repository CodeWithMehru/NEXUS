# 🎬 NEXUS AI — Demo Video Script (2–4 minutes)

**Goal:** show, don't tell. Prove three modes work, that the AI is *grounded and honest*, and that it solves a real problem for the **brains of tomorrow**.

**Before you record:**
- Set `GROQ_API_KEY` (and ideally `TAVILY_API_KEY` so the *"Grounded by live web search"* badge + Sources appear).
- Pre-load one real business name (e.g. *Zomato*) and one startup idea so there's no typing dead-air.
- Record at 1080p+, dark room, system audio off. Keep cursor movements slow and deliberate.

**Total runtime target: ~3:00.** Timings below are a guide — trim to fit 2–4 min.

---

### 0:00 – 0:20 · Hook (Landing)
**Screen:** Landing page hero. Cursor hovers the headline *"Will your business survive the next 5 years?"*
**Voiceover:**
> "Meet NEXUS AI — antifragile intelligence for tomorrow's businesses and young founders. Built for Brainwave 2026. Three AI modes, one mission: see the next disruption *before* it hits."

---

### 0:20 – 1:00 · Nexus Mode — live, grounded intelligence
**Screen:** Type `Zomato` (or click the chip) → **Run Analysis**. Loading messages cycle → the report animates in.
**Point at, in order:** the **FutureProof Score** ring → the green **"Grounded by live web search"** badge → **AI Disruption Risks** bars → **Market Threats/Opportunities** → scroll to **Sources** (clickable, real URLs).
**Voiceover:**
> "Type any business. In seconds, NEXUS scores how future-proof it is — and grounds its market signals in *live web results*, with real sources. No key? It says so honestly and falls back to AI inference. It never fabricates numbers."

---

### 1:00 – 1:55 · Vanguard Mode — Command Center + Oracle + Cyber
**Screen:** Click **Open Command Center**. Drag a sample `sales.csv` into **The Vault** → click **Run Full Internal Scan**.
**Point at:** Oracle's response — **P0/P1/P2 priority chips**, **effort** badges, the **"Watch this metric"** KPI line, and the **confidence** badge.
**Voiceover:**
> "Upload your real data. The Oracle Agent quotes *your* numbers verbatim and returns prioritized, effort-rated actions — plus the one metric to watch next."

**Then:** Scroll to **Scenario Simulator** → hit the **Cyber Threat** panic button.
**Point at:** the before/after chart dropping, then the **4-step mitigation plan** and the amber **"India compliance clock: CERT-In 6h · DPDP Act breach notice"** callout.
**Voiceover:**
> "Pull a panic button — say, a ransomware breach. NEXUS simulates the 90-day hit *and* generates a compliance-grade response on India's real regulatory clock: CERT-In within six hours, DPDP Act breach notification. That's not generic advice — that's the law."

---

### 1:55 – 2:40 · Young Founder Mode — Brains of Tomorrow
**Screen:** Click **Young Founder** in the nav (or the green CTA). Type an idea, e.g. *"AI tutor for NEET aspirants in regional languages"*, pick stage **Idea** → **Validate Idea**.
**Point at:** the **Validation Score** ring → **Risk Flags** → the **Lean Launch Roadmap** (2 weeks / 1 month / 3 months) → **Funding Strategy** (Startup India, grants) → **First 10 Customers** → **AI Leverage**.
**Voiceover:**
> "Now the part built for the brains of tomorrow. A student describes an idea — and gets an honest validation score, a lean roadmap, a bootstrap-first funding plan, the AI tools to move ten times faster, and their first ten customers. Six months of trial-and-error, in thirty seconds."

---

### 2:40 – 3:00 · Privacy + Close
**Screen:** Back in Command Center, hover the **shield icon** to reveal the privacy tooltip; then cut to the landing footer.
**Voiceover:**
> "Sensitive data? Keys stay server-side, uploads stay in your session, nothing's sold or used to train models — and Reset wipes it all. NEXUS AI: making today's businesses and tomorrow's founders antifragile. Built for Brainwave 2026."
**End card:** logo + live demo URL + *"Empowering Brains of Tomorrow."*

---

### 🎙️ Delivery tips
- **Energy + clarity** beat fancy editing. Speak ~10% faster than feels natural.
- Let one number animate fully before you move on — the motion *is* the wow.
- If a live call is slow, pre-record that segment or use the deterministic fallbacks (simulator/alerts work without keys).
- Caption the regulation names on-screen (**CERT-In**, **DPDP Act 2023**, **Startup India**) — judges remember specifics.
